# Funding and Support

The Emergency Publishing System is designed to provide secure, censorship-resistant communication for people in high-risk environments.  
Funding helps sustain development, security reviews, and long-term maintenance.

---

## Project Goals

- Provide safe communication under censorship  
- Protect user anonymity  
- Deliver reliable information during crises  
- Support regions with unstable connectivity  

---

## How Funding Helps

Funding supports:
- Security audits  
- Transport obfuscation research  
- Regional deployment testing  
- Documentation and operator guides  
- Long-term maintenance  

---

## Funding Principles

- Transparency  
- Security-first development  
- No collection of personal data  
- No commercial exploitation  

---
