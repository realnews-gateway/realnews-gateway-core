# Code of Conduct

This project aims to create a safe, respectful, and collaborative environment for contributors and users.

---

## Our Standards

We commit to:
- Respectful communication  
- Constructive feedback  
- Inclusive participation  
- Professional behavior  

We do not tolerate:
- Harassment  
- Discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Threats or intimidation  

---

## Responsibilities

Contributors must:
- Maintain a respectful tone  
- Consider security implications  
- Protect user safety  
- Follow contribution guidelines  

Maintainers must:
- Review contributions fairly  
- Enforce this Code of Conduct  
- Address unacceptable behavior  

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Report privately  
- Provide relevant details  
- Anonymous reports are allowed  

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warnings  
- Temporary restrictions  
- Permanent removal from the project  

---

## Commitment

We strive to maintain a safe and supportive environment for everyone involved.
